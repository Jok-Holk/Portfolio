THIEF is a 2.5D fighting game developed with Unity, focusing on combat mechanics, player controls, and enemy behavior in a side-scrolling environment.
<br>
I was responsible for most of the gameplay development, including combat systems, enemy AI, combat flow design, and UI/UX implementation using <b>Unity</b> and <b>C#</b>. The project strengthened my understanding of gameplay logic and system-oriented design in fighting games.
